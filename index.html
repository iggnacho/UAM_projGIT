    Website User Manual

    How to use the Map

    Click on any marker on the map to open up information about the point of interest, or a list of points of interest within a near radius(cluster). If clicked on cluster of markers, you will receive a cluster report in the overlay. Click on any link within the list to continue to the information panel for that point of interest. If there is a related artists graph available, a button will display under the information panel to open up the graph overlay.

    How to use the Related Artists Graph

    The overlay graph is designed to be explored. Click on the artist name nodes to see if they have any other events in the dataset. Once an event or an artist expands you can always click on them to collapse their networks. The map in the top left corner shows the locations of the events being displayed in the network and will automatically update as you explore and expand the graph.

    Working with Time

    The map has the ability to filter the selection of data being displayed by date ranges and also the ability to show you how the influences have changed year-to-year by pressing the play button. The time bar chart can be displayed upon clicking the up button by the time displayed at the footer of the map application. This allows for custom year range selections through click and dragging the years desired to display.
